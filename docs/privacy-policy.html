Manakin — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Manakin ("the app") is a field companion for exploring species phenology — when and where plants and animals are most likely to be observed. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. The app is operated by Cody Limber ("we", "the developer").

Summary

  • We do not have user accounts, and we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data on our own servers.
  • The app has no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs.
  • The app does not request or use your device's GPS or precise location.
  • Your settings and downloaded data stay on your device.

Information the app handles

  • Optional iNaturalist username. If you choose to enter your iNaturalist username in Settings, it is stored locally on your device and sent to the public iNaturalist API to retrieve your publicly available observation data (for example, to show which species you have already seen). Entering a username is optional and the app works without it. We never ask for, receive, or store your iNaturalist password.
  • Place and species searches. When you build a data pack or search for a location or taxon, the text you type is sent to the public iNaturalist API to return matching results. These are text queries; the app does not use device location services.
  • App data stored on your device. Downloaded species data packs, your favorites/targets, favorite taxa, and app preferences are saved locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the app.

Third-party services

To function, the app makes network requests to the following third parties. We do not control their data practices; please review their own policies.

  • iNaturalist (api.inaturalist.org) — species data, observation maps, place/taxon search, and species photos. See iNaturalist's privacy policy.
  • CARTO (basemaps.cartocdn.com) — base map tiles for the observation map. See CARTO's privacy policy.

As with any internet request, these services may receive your device's IP address and the content of the request (for example, the search term or the map area being viewed) in order to respond. Map data is © OpenStreetMap contributors, © CARTO.

Permissions

  • Internet — to download species data, maps, and photos.
  • Notifications — optional, to deliver the weekly digest and target-species reminders you enable in Settings. These notifications are generated on your device.
  • Foreground service (data sync) — to keep large data-pack downloads running reliably while the app is generating a dataset.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
